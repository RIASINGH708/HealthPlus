Diabetes

Hypertension

High blood pressure often has no symptoms. Over time, if untreated, it can cause health conditions, such as heart disease and stroke

PTSD

Symptoms may include nightmares or flashbacks, avoidance of situations that bring back the trauma, heightened reactivity to stimuli, anxiety or depressed mood.agitation, irritability, hostility, hypervigilance, self-destructive behaviour, or social isolation

cough and cold

It usually begins with a sore throat, and before you know it, you've also got these symptoms: Runny nose (clear and watery) Sneezing Fatigue Cough You usually don't get a fever with a cold. If you do, it may be a sign you've got the flu or an infection with a bacteria.
